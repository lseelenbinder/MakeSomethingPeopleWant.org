What?

makesomethingpeoplewant.org provides a simple, non-intrusive page of carefully-chosen, timeless quotes—one at a time.

Why?

We believe that every person has work to do, and we know the struggle to accomplish that work with the noise of the Internet.

So…

We designed makesomethingpeoplewant.org to help restore focus. The simplicity doesn't distract; the quotes motivate.

How?

  • You could set makesomethingpeoplewant.org as your homepage.
  • You could try our chrome extension to block out the distracting sites.
